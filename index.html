about

I am currently a second-year MSc Immunology Student at HMS and a member of the Garcia-Beltran iNK Lab at the Ragon Institute of Harvard, MIT, and MGH. My work is focused on chemokines/cell homing in the tumour microenvironment, NK cell control of mpxv + SARS-CoV-2, and 3D culturing techniques.

Previously, I completed my BSc in Biomedical Sciences and Drug Mechanisms at UCL. My BSc dissertation project at the Reeve's Lab was on the induction of tissue residency in circulating NK cells. Prior to that, I was a research assistant at the Biozone Lab for Metabolic Systems Engineering at the University of Toronto's Department of Chemical Engineering.
