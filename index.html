about

I am currently a second-year MMSc Immunology Student at HMS and a member of the Garcia-Beltran iNK Lab at the Ragon Institute of Harvard, MIT, and MGH. My work is focused on optimising CAR NK function, chemokines in the tumour microenvironment, and 3D culturing techniques.

Previously, I completed my BSc in Biomedical Sciences and Drug Mechanisms at UCL. My BSc dissertation project at the Reeve's Lab was on the induction of adaptive-like and tissue resident traits in circulating NK cells in the context of HCMV infection. Prior to that, I was a research assistant at the Biozone Lab for Metabolic Systems Engineering at the University of Toronto's Department of Chemical Engineering. Feel free to email me with any questions about my current or past projects.
